Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

RPMR Creations (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the YayOrNay mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the App.

A. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed and not accessible in readable form)

Passwords are encrypted and stored using secure authentication services.

B. Profile Information

After registering, you may provide:

  • A user-selected display name

This display name is used for in-app identification and does not need to be your real name.

C. Voting Activity

We store:

  • Records of which account voted on which poll

This ensures poll integrity and prevents duplicate voting.

D. Friend Connections

If you add another user as a friend:

  • We store the email address used to send the friend request
  • We store confirmation of accepted connections

There is currently no direct in-app messaging.

E. Future Commenting Features

If commenting is introduced in the future:

  • We may store comments submitted by users
  • Comments may be visible to other users within the App

Comment data will be stored in connection with your account and display name.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Real names (unless voluntarily used as display names)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Payment information
  • Contacts from your device
  • Analytics tracking data

We do not sell or monetize user data.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users
  • Enable participation in polls
  • Prevent duplicate voting
  • Manage friend connections
  • Maintain system integrity and security
  • Improve app functionality

We do not use your information for advertising or profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR – EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

Contractual Necessity

We process your email and voting activity to provide the App’s core functionality (account access and poll participation).

Legitimate Interests

We process limited data to:

  • Prevent abuse
  • Maintain system security
  • Ensure fair voting mechanisms

Consent

Where required by law, we rely on user consent (for example, if new features require additional data collection).

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using Supabase, a secure backend infrastructure provider.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure authentication
  • Password hashing
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)

While we take security seriously, no system is completely immune to risk.

6. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Supabase (database and authentication services)

Supabase may process data on our behalf in accordance with their privacy practices.

We do not use advertising networks, behavioral tracking services, or third-party analytics tools.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only while your account remains active.

If you delete your account, the following will be permanently deleted from active systems (except where retention is required by law):

  • Your email address
  • Display name
  • Vote records
  • Friend connections
  • Any associated comments (if applicable)

8. Account Deletion

Users may delete their account directly within the App.

Upon deletion:

  • All associated personal data is permanently removed.

No email request is required.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such data without parental consent, we will delete it.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact: admin@rpmrcreations.com

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for processing
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact: admin@rpmrcreations.com

We will respond within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable law.

11. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate.

By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by a revised “Last Updated” date.

Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

13. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: admin@rpmrcreations.com

Important Compliance Notes

Note: This policy applies to the YayOrNay mobile application.
