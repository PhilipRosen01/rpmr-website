How It Works

Four Simple Steps

Create a puzzle in seconds, then sit back and watch your friends try to figure it out.

1
📸

Snap a Photo

Take a photo or pick one from your gallery — anything from pets to landmarks to inside jokes.

2
🔤

Set the Clue

Choose a one or two word clue that describes your image. Pick your grid size from 3×3 up to 20×20.

3
📨

Send to Friends

Pick one friend or a whole group. They'll get a notification that a new puzzle is waiting.

4
🧩

Reveal & Guess

Tiles reveal on your press. Guess the word early for bonus points — or wait for more clues!

Features

Everything You Need to Play

Built for fun, designed for friends.

🖼️

Custom Grid Sizes

Choose 3×3 for a quick game, or go up to 20×20 for a real challenge. Scramble mode shuffles the tiles for extra difficulty.

👥

Group Puzzles

Send the same puzzle to multiple friends at once. Everyone shares the same reveal — race to guess first!

⚡

Difficulty Presets

Easy, Medium, Hard, or fully Custom. Control reveal speed, batch size, and daily reveal timing.

🏆

Points & Badges

Earn points for early guesses. Level up through Solver, Creator, and Collector badges as you play.

🔔

Push Notifications

Get notified when friends send puzzles, new tiles reveal, or someone solves your creation.

🔗

Share & Deep Links

Share puzzles with a link that opens directly in the app. Works across iOS, Android, and Web.

📊

Puzzle Monitor

Track who's solving your puzzle in real-time. Grant extra guesses, reveal hints, or toggle scramble.

🔒

Safe & Private

Block & report users, profanity filtering, age verification, and end-to-end encrypted auth tokens.
