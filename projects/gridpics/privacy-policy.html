Privacy Policy

RPMR Creations ("we," "us," or "our") operates the GridPics mobile application and website at puzzle.rpmrcreations.com (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

By using the Service you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not collect contacts or address book information.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service — create and manage your account, deliver puzzles, manage friendships, display leaderboards and stats.
  • Communicate with you — send push notifications about new puzzles, friend requests, and puzzle progress (only if you opt in).
  • Safety & moderation — enforce our Terms of Service, review reports of abusive content, and maintain a safe community.
  • Improve the Service — understand how features are used so we can fix bugs and improve the experience.

3. How We Store & Protect Your Data

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (hosted on AWS) with the following safeguards:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) using Expo SecureStore — not in plain-text storage.
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access their own data.
  • Uploaded images are stored in private Supabase Storage buckets with access controls.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users — your username, avatar, points, and puzzle stats are visible to friends and puzzle recipients. Your email address and date of birth are never shared with other users.
  • Service providers — we use Supabase for hosting and data storage, and Apple Push Notification Service / Expo Push for delivering notifications. These providers process data solely on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

5. Your Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Update

You can view and update your username, email, avatar, and date of birth at any time in the app's Settings screen.

5.2 Push Notifications

Push notifications are opt-in. You can enable or disable them at any time through your device's system settings.

5.3 Block & Report

You can block any user from your Friends screen. Blocking a user removes all traces of your account from their view, including puzzles, friend connections, and notifications. You can unblock users at any time.

5.4 Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. When you delete your account:

  • Your profile, puzzles, stats, badges, and friendships are permanently removed.
  • All uploaded images (avatars and puzzle images) are deleted from our storage.
  • Your authentication credentials are destroyed.
  • This action is irreversible.

5.5 Data Portability

If you would like a copy of your personal data, please contact us at the email below and we will provide it within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

GridPics is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We enforce a minimum age of 13 at registration through date-of-birth verification. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete the account and associated data. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed immediately as described in Section 5.4. We may retain anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., total puzzle count) that cannot identify you.

By using the Service, you consent to such transfer. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
